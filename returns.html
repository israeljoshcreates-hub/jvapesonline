Return & Refund Policy

Effective date: December 9, 2025

Thank you for shopping with J_VAPES.KE. This Return & Refund Policy explains how returns, exchanges, and refunds are handled for purchases made from our website.

1. Eligibility for Returns

A. Wrong item received

If you receive the wrong product, you may return it within 3 days of delivery for an exchange or refund.

B. Damaged or defective items

If your item arrives damaged or stops working within 7 days of delivery, we will replace it at no cost. You must provide a photo/video of the damage or defect, plus your order number and contact details.

C. Non-returnable items

For safety and hygiene reasons, the following items cannot be returned or refunded: opened e-liquids, used disposable vapes, used coils/pods, any product that has been tampered with, or items damaged due to misuse or improper handling.

2. Conditions for return approval

To be eligible, items must be unused and in original packaging, in the same condition as received, and returned with proof of purchase (receipt, order number). Returns that do not meet these conditions may be rejected.

3. Return process

Contact us on WhatsApp at +254 741 658 556 within the return window. Provide photos, videos, or a detailed explanation of the issue. Our team will review and approve or reject your return request. Approved returns must be sent back to the address we provide. Once received and inspected, we will issue a replacement or refund.

4. Refunds

Refunds are issued using your original payment method. Refund timeline: Refund approval: 1–2 business days. Processing by payment provider: 3–7 business days. Shipping fees are not refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item or defective product).

5. Exchanges

If you want to exchange an item for a different variant (flavour, colour, device type), the item must be unused and in its original condition. Price differences may apply.

6. Order cancellations

Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of purchase as long as they have not yet been shipped. After shipping, the order cannot be cancelled but may still be eligible for a return under the conditions above.

7. Lost or missing parcels

If your parcel is marked as delivered but you did not receive it: contact us immediately on WhatsApp; we will investigate with the courier. Replacement or refund will be handled on a case-by-case basis. We are not responsible for parcels lost due to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

8. Contact information

For returns, exchanges, or refund inquiries, contact us on WhatsApp: +254 741 658 556. Company: J_VAPES.KE. Address: Nairobi, Kenya.
